## [Instrumental Introduction: Light Drum Roll]

## [Verse 1]

### [Tenor Soloist]
We are the builders, we are the dreamers.  
We are the sovereigns, who build the shield strong.

## [Verse 2]

### [Baritone Soloist]
Her grace is our healer, and a strong provider.  
The will of the beaver, a spirit to behold.

## [Verse 3]

### [Bass Soloist]
Her vast green fortress, built by master craftsmen.  
This dam of abundance, is our future home.

## [Chorus]

### [Victorious, Choral Harmony]
This place called the Hudson, the land of the sovereigns.  
Fortress of the beaver, woven by hand and soul.  
This place is provider, we are the stewards.  
She is our grace and glory, a Republic for all.

This place called the Hudson, the land of the sovereigns.  
We are nature's builders, who raise and who grow her.  
Through enduring spirit, we forge our own future.  
In her jubilant glory, the Hudsoners stand tall.

## [Verse 4]

### [Mezzo-Soprano Soloist]
From the deep Red River, came stoic survivors.  
The kingdom of sovereigns, who are skilled and strong.

## [Verse 5]

### [Alto Soloist]
Our fearless, trucking spirit, endures the biting winter.  
A rally of the fallen, lifts a veil for all.

## [Verse 6]

### [Soprano Soloist]
From Crown exploitation, rose a beaver nation.  
The Hudson's beauty, is a Republic's freedom call.

## [Bridge: Light Snare Roll]

### [Hands Clapping]

## [Chorus]

### [Marching Band, Massive Choral Chanting]
This place called the Hudson, the land of the sovereigns.  
Fortress of the beaver, woven by hand and soul.  
We will create her, through our immense labor.  
Her beauty will be built, and mended by all.

This place called the Hudson, the land of the sovereigns.  
Fortress of the beaver, woven by hand and soul.  
Now we the people, the sovereigns who stand tall.  
We must seize our future, in order to not fall.

### [Hook: Claps, Flutes]

This watershed moment, will define us all!
